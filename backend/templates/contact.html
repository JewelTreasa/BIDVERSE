Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I get started with BidVerse?

Simply register as a farmer or buyer, complete your profile verification, and start participating in auctions immediately.

Is my payment secure?

Yes, we use bank-grade security and escrow-like payment systems to protect both buyers and sellers throughout the transaction.

How are auctions conducted?

Auctions run for a set time period. Place your bids in real-time, and the highest bidder wins when the auction ends.

What commodities can I trade?

We support a wide range of agricultural commodities including grains, spices, pulses, and cash crops from verified farmers.
