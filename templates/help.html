Help Center

Find answers to frequently asked questions about BidVerse.

General FAQ

What is BidVerse?

BidVerse is a digital auction platform connecting agricultural producers directly with buyers.

How do I join?

You can register as a Buyer or Seller using the "Get Started" button in the navigation bar.

Bidding Help

How do I place a bid?

Go to the Marketplace, find a live auction, and enter your bid amount on the auction detail page.

Can I cancel a bid?

Bids are binding commitments and cannot be cancelled once placed.

Safety & Security

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use advanced encryption to protect your personal and transaction data.

How are users verified?

Our administrators verify seller identities to ensure high quality and trust on the platform.
